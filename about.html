About

Bronx (NYC) based photographer & creative partner.

As a visual journalist and portrait artist rooted deep in the cultural fabric of The Bronx, New York, my work is driven by the unique human stories that make NYC vibrant. My photographic career covers editorial campaigns, professional executive branding, and artistic profile development for artists, activists, and leaders.

I believe a great photograph doesn't just display your features—it speaks to your authority, your vulnerability, and your story. Combining extensive corporate strategic insights with professional camera and light management, I bring a structured, pleasant, and premium creative solution straight to your office, studio, or local Bronx park.
